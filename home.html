Exhiben

The web based animal exhibition managing platform. Visit Kickstarter

Which are the greatest things about it?

Access from anywhere Because Exhiben is a web platform you and your exhibitors can get access from anywhere at any time.
Easy colaboration Devide organisational tasks between members without complications or risks!
Editable Submissions Submissions can be edited up to the date of closing for new submissions. Any difference in submission fee will be processed upon closing.
Direct payment Exhibitors pay directly to your organisation by Mollie integration. So Exhiben does not charge any payment fees.
Reliable The Exhiben infrastructure is highly reliable so your data will be stored safely.
User friendly Exhiben is easy to use for organisations and exhibitors. It will save organisations time manually processing submissions and payments.
